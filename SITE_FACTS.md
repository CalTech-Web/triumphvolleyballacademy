**Tagline / Value Prop:** "Faith. Community. Excellence."

**One-liner:** Triumph Volleyball Academy (TVA) is a Christian-based volleyball club in the Central Valley of California offering competitive boys indoor, girls indoor, and sand volleyball programs for youth athletes.

**Founding Story / Category:** Faith-Based Youth Volleyball Club - Founded to develop athletes holistically - in sport, character, and faith. TVA believes athletics is a platform to impact the world for Jesus Christ.

## Vision & Mission

**Vision:**
"To see the world impacted for Jesus Christ through the influence of athletes and coaches and the sport of volleyball."

**Mission:**
Develop student athletes physically, mentally, and spiritually through high-quality volleyball training in a Christian environment.

**Core Bible Verse:**
"Whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God." - 1 Corinthians 10:31
